Les Mills Bodycombat

VR Meta Quest Live Service

Les Mills Bodycombat is a Virtual Reality live service fitness game based on the widely acclaimed Bodycombat training program, released for Meta Quest and PlayStation VR using Unity.

  • Formed part of the team working on the transition to the new season pass service model.
  • Implemented new gameplay features, a reworked UI and a new unit testing framework.
  • Worked on the game’s soft reboot: refactoring the whole codebase, implementing new architecture and design patterns, and applying Scrum to meet target deadlines.

Dani & Evan: Dinosaur Books

Visual Novel Android/iOS Nintendo Switch

Dani & Evan: Dinosaur Books is an interactive visual novel based on the book series "The Adventures of Dani and Evan", developed using Unity.

  • Programmed and designed the game, releasing it for Android/iOS and later porting it to the Nintendo Switch as well as developing a freemium version.
  • As part of a small team of four, I took responsibility for the technical side of the project, ensuring we met the deadlines required by investors.
  • Designed the GDD and the game’s architecture, implementing the necessary design patterns and optimisations to ensure the project ran smoothly on all devices.
  • Also took on the roles of animator, VFX artist, scriptwriter and audio designer, using FMOD Studio for the latter.

Broken Echoes

Action/Adventure PC Indie

Broken Echoes is an independent project developed alongside 6 colleagues using Unity. It's an action/adventure game with different combat classes, exploration and motion captured cutscenes.

  • Took on the role of Game Programmer, focusing on UI and audio integration.
  • Recorded cutscenes with motion capture technology using an Xbox Kinect.
  • Used FMOD Studio to implemented dynamic SFX and ambience, adaptive music and 3D audio. Also composed the game’s 9 music tracks. Here's "The Broken Continuum", the game's battle theme.

The Vanishing Hitchhiker

Visual Novel Nintendo Switch

The Vanishing Hitchhiker is a short visual novel for the Nintendo Switch with multiple story paths, different endings and spooky girls.

  • Programmed and designed the game, coordinating with scriptwriters and the client to implement the game's branching story paths.
  • I created a custom markup language to handle the game's events and branching paths. It would later be reused in future projects.
  • The game was developed from scratch for the Nintendo Switch.

Pinzo's Smoothie Stand

Puzzle PC Prototype

Pinzo's Smoothie Stand is my own personal project, developed as a way to learn about 2.5D environments, editor tooling and puzzle generation. I worked on it as a solo developer, with some help for character sprites and models.

  • In this story-driven game, Pinzo the crab makes smoothies for his clients, but also helps them with their problems along the way!
  • The game's puzzles consist on Pinzo rolling fruits from the beach to his stand under a number of turns, avoiding obstacles and the ever threatening tide.
  • The game features a 2.5D artstyle, with cut-out characters on a 3D realistic world.
  • Maybe someday I will finish and release it!
